Petcare Tips

  • Keep
    Fresh
    Water:     Always make sure your pet has clean water. Remember to check and fill their water bowl every day.
  • Feed
    On
    Time:     Stick to a regular feeding schedule for your pet. It's important not to give them too much food.
  • Play
    Every
    Day:     Spend time with your pet each day, whether it's playing fetch or just hanging out.
  • Groom
    Clean
    Love:     Show your pet some love by grooming them. Brush their fur and keep them clean.
  • Make
    Home
    Safe:     Keep your home safe for your pet. Make sure things like toys or foods that can hurt them are out of reach.
  • Check
    With
    Vet:     Visit the vet regularly to make sure your pet is healthy. Vets help keep our pets happy and well.
  • Tag
    For
    Safety:     If your pet goes outside, put a tag on their collar. It's like a name tag that keeps them safe.
